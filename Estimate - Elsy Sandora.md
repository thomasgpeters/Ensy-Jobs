## Sub-Job 1: Gazebo with Patio

**Scope:** Install a 10x12 gazebo kit on existing patio area.

**Installation Plan:**
- Anchor the gazebo using short 4x4 posts, leveled and squared on the ground in the correct position
- May need to remove up to 4 patio blocks (pavers) to accommodate one of the anchor posts
- Replace/reset any disturbed pavers after post placement

**Site Conditions (from photos):**
- Existing brick paver patio in fair condition (some moss/aging)
- Gravel border area adjacent to patio with landscape edging
- Gazebo kit materials on site (boxes stacked against house wall)
- Ivy/ground cover encroaching from gravel side — may need trimming back for clearance

**Materials:**
- 10x12 Gazebo kit (on site)
- 4x4 pressure-treated posts (short — for anchoring)
- Hardware/fasteners for anchoring
- Concrete (if setting posts)

**Labor:**
- Layout and positioning of anchor points
- Remove patio blocks as needed
- Set and level 4x4 anchor posts
- Assemble gazebo kit per manufacturer instructions
- Reinstall/patch any displaced pavers
