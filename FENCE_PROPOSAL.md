# Fence with Gate Replacement Proposal

**Client:** Elsy Sandora
**Date:** April 16, 2026

---

## Project Overview

Remove the existing worn white-painted wood fence and gate. Replace with new posts and prefab vinyl-clad fence sections at approximately the same height, including a matching vinyl-clad gate.

---

## Existing Conditions

- Current fence is built from 2x4s, white painted, with horizontal slat sections and a vertical picket gate
- Paint is badly peeling and wood is very worn throughout
- Posts are deteriorating at the base
- Gate connects the side yard to the backyard patio area
- Stepping stones run through the gate opening

---

## Scope of Work

### 1. Demolition and Removal

- Take down all existing fence sections and gate
- Remove old posts from the ground
- Haul away and dispose of all old fence materials

### 2. Post Setting (6 Posts)

- Lay out and mark 6 post locations in a straight line
- Dig post holes to proper depth
- Set 6 posts in concrete
- Level and plumb each post
- String-line all posts to confirm they are aligned in a straight line
- Allow concrete to cure before attaching fence sections

### 3. Fence Section Installation

- Measure and confirm prefab vinyl-clad fence sections fit between posts
- Install fence sections at approximately the same height as the existing fence
- Secure sections to posts with appropriate brackets and fasteners
- Ensure all sections are level and aligned

### 4. Gate Installation

- Install a matching vinyl-clad gate in the existing gate opening
- Mount gate hinges and latch hardware
- Adjust gate for proper swing, clearance, and secure closure
